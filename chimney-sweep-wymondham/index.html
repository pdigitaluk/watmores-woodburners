Common questions

Wymondham Chimney Sweep FAQs

How often should I have my chimney swept in Wymondham?

At least once a year for wood burners and solid fuel appliances. Most UK home insurers require this annually. We issue a sweep certificate after every visit.

Do you cover the villages around Wymondham?

Yes — Hethersett, Spooner Row, Wicklewood, Kimberley, Barnham Broom, Bawburgh, Long Stratton and all surrounding south Norfolk villages are within our regular service area.

Do I need a sweep certificate for my home insurance?

Most UK home insurers require evidence of annual sweeping for solid fuel properties. We issue an official certificate on the day of every visit.

How long does a chimney sweep take in Wymondham?

Around 45–60 minutes. We use dust sheets throughout and leave your home completely clean.

Can you sweep both a liner and an open fireplace?

Yes — we sweep all flue types including stainless steel flexible liners, clay flue pots and original brick chimney flues for wood and solid fuel appliances.
