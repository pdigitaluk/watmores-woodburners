How much does a woodburner installation cost in Fakenham?

Do you install log burners in the villages around Fakenham?

My Fakenham property has an old open fireplace — can you fit a stove?

In most cases, yes. An existing fireplace is often ideal for a wood burning stove installation. We assess the flue condition and dimensions during your free survey and advise on the best approach — this is included at no cost.

Do you need planning permission for a woodburner in Fakenham?

In most cases, no planning permission is required. Installation falls under permitted development. As HETAS registered installers, we handle building regulations notification on your behalf as part of every installation.

How long does a woodburner installation take in Fakenham?

Most installations are completed in a single day. We arrive, complete all work cleanly and efficiently, and leave your home tidy. Complex jobs involving a twin-wall external flue may take two days — we always confirm the timescale upfront.
