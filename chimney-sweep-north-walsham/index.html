Chimney Sweeping in North Walsham

We provide a professional, reliable chimney sweeping service in North Walsham and throughout north Norfolk. Whether you have a wood burning stove, multi-fuel stove, open fireplace or oil boiler flue, we carry out a thorough clean and inspection using professional brushes and equipment — and issue a sweep certificate on the day.

Local property types

Properties We Sweep in North Walsham

Georgian and Victorian town centre

North Walsham's market town core includes some elegant period properties with large multi-flue chimney systems. Original clay liners in these properties can be fragile and benefit from careful annual cleaning.

Post-war semis and bungalows

Much of North Walsham's housing stock dates from the 1950s–1970s, built with solid fuel fireplaces as standard. Many were converted to gas and then back again — these transitional flues often need a thorough clean after years of mixed use.

Coastal villages and surrounding area

Mundesley, Happisburgh, Walcott and the Poppyland coast villages are all within our regular North Walsham run — usually combined on the same day to give quick availability across the whole area.

Common questions

North Walsham Chimney Sweep FAQs

How often should I have my chimney swept in North Walsham?

At least once a year for wood burners and open fires. Some insurers specify twice a year for heavily used appliances. We issue a certificate after every sweep.

Do you sweep chimneys in the villages around North Walsham?

Yes — Stalham, Aylsham, Coltishall, Wroxham, Mundesley and all surrounding north Norfolk villages are within our regular service area.

Do I need a chimney sweep certificate for my home insurance?

Most UK home insurers require evidence of annual chimney sweeping for properties with solid fuel appliances. We issue an official sweep certificate on the day of every visit.

How long does a chimney sweep take in North Walsham?

A standard chimney sweep takes around 45–60 minutes. We use dust sheets throughout and leave your home completely clean and tidy.

Can you sweep a flue liner as well as an open fireplace?

Yes — we sweep all flue types including stainless steel flexible liners, rigid liners, clay flue pots and open brick chimneys for both solid fuel and wood appliances.
