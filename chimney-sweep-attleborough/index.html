Common questions

Attleborough Chimney Sweep FAQs

How often should I have my chimney swept in Attleborough?

At least once a year for wood burners and solid fuel appliances. Most UK home insurers require this annually. We issue a sweep certificate after every visit.

Do you cover Old Buckenham and Wymondham from Attleborough?

Yes — Old Buckenham, New Buckenham, Wymondham, Morley and all surrounding south Norfolk villages are within our regular service area.

Do I need a sweep certificate for my home insurance in Attleborough?

Most UK home insurers require evidence of annual chimney sweeping for solid fuel properties. We issue an official certificate on the day of every visit.

How long does a chimney sweep take in Attleborough?

Around 45–60 minutes. We use dust sheets throughout and leave your home completely clean.

Can you sweep both a stove liner and an open fireplace?

Yes — we sweep all flue types including stainless steel flexible liners, clay flue pots and original brick chimney flues.
