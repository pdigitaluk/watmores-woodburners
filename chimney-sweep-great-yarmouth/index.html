Great Yarmouth Chimney Sweep FAQs

How often should I have my chimney swept in Great Yarmouth?

At least once a year for wood burners and solid fuel appliances. Most home insurers require annual evidence of sweeping. We issue a sweep certificate after every visit.

Do you cover Gorleston and Caister from Great Yarmouth?

Yes — Gorleston, Caister-on-Sea, Bradwell, Belton, Acle and all surrounding east Norfolk villages are within our regular service area.

Do I need a sweep certificate for my home insurance in Great Yarmouth?

Most UK home insurers require evidence of annual chimney sweeping for solid fuel properties. We issue an official certificate on the day of every visit.

How long does a chimney sweep take in Great Yarmouth?

Around 45–60 minutes. We use dust sheets throughout and leave your home completely clean.

Can you sweep a stove liner as well as an open fireplace in Great Yarmouth?

Yes — we sweep all flue types including stainless steel flexible liners, clay flue pots and original brick chimney flues.
