Do you need a hearth?

A Hearth Isn't Optional

Under UK building regulations, every wood burning stove must be installed on a non-combustible hearth. It's not just a design choice — it's a legal requirement. The hearth protects your floor from heat and stray embers, and must meet minimum size requirements based on your stove.

01

Building regulation requirement

All wood burning stove installations in the UK must include a non-combustible hearth that meets minimum size requirements — typically 300mm in front of the stove door and 150mm to each side.

02

Supplied & fitted in one visit

Common questions

Hearth & Surround FAQs

Everything you need to know about hearth supply and fitting in Norfolk.

Do I need a hearth for a wood burning stove?

Yes — UK building regulations require every wood burning stove to sit on a non-combustible hearth. It must extend a minimum of 300mm in front of the stove door and 150mm to either side. A tiled hearth is one of the most popular options and something we supply and fit as standard across Norwich and Norfolk.

What type of hearth is best for a wood burning stove?

It depends on your property style and taste. Slate effect hearths are the most popular choice in Norfolk — durable, timeless, and suited to both traditional and modern interiors. For contemporary homes, porcelain or concrete effect hearths are increasingly popular. We'll advise you during your free home survey.

Is hearth fitting included in your stove installation?

Yes — we supply and fit hearths and fireplace beams as part of your stove installation package. Everything is completed in a single visit and included in your written quote from the outset. No need to source anything separately.

Can you fit a fireplace beam to a non-standard size?

Yes — we fit fireplace beams to both standard and non-standard sizes. Whether your fireplace opening is a common width or requires a bespoke cut, we can accommodate it. We measure up during your free survey and advise on the best option for your space.

How much does a tiled hearth cost in Norfolk?

Hearth costs vary by style, size, and fitting complexity. We include hearth supply and fitting in your overall stove installation quote so you receive one clear written price. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote.

What areas in Norfolk do you cover?
