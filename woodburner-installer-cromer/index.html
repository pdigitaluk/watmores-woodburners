Cromer Woodburner FAQs

How much does a woodburner installation cost in Cromer?

A fully installed wood burning stove in Cromer starts from £1,926 with Watmore's Woodburners. This covers everything — stove supply, flue liner, hearth preparation, HETAS certificate and building regulations notification. We provide a free written quote before any work starts.

Are Victorian properties in Cromer suitable for woodburners?

Yes — Cromer's Victorian and Edwardian properties are often ideal. Many have original chimney flues that just need a new liner to safely accept a modern stove. We assess every property during your free survey and advise on the most suitable solution.

Do you cover Sheringham and Holt from Cromer?

Yes — Sheringham, Holt, Overstrand, West Runton, East Runton and all surrounding north Norfolk coast villages are within our regular service area. No extra travel charge applies.

What HETAS certification do I receive?

You receive an official HETAS certificate and building regulations notification. These are required by home insurers and confirm your installation meets all UK safety and building standards.

Most installations are completed in a single day. We arrive, complete all work cleanly and efficiently, and leave your home tidy. Complex jobs involving a twin-wall external flue may take two days.
