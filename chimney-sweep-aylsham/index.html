Common questions

Aylsham Chimney Sweep FAQs

How often should I have my chimney swept in Aylsham?

At least once a year for wood burners and solid fuel appliances. Most UK home insurers require this annually. We issue a sweep certificate after every visit.

Do you cover Blickling and Coltishall from Aylsham?

Yes — Blickling, Coltishall, Buxton, Brampton, Marsham, Oulton and all surrounding north Norfolk villages are within our regular service area.

Do I need a sweep certificate for my home insurance in Aylsham?

Most UK home insurers require evidence of annual chimney sweeping for properties with solid fuel appliances. We issue an official certificate on the day of every visit.

How long does a chimney sweep take in Aylsham?

Around 45–60 minutes. We use dust sheets throughout and leave your home completely clean and tidy.

Can you sweep both a stove liner and an open fireplace in Aylsham?

Yes — we sweep all flue types including stainless steel flexible liners, clay flue pots and original brick chimney flues.
