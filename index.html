Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to install a wood burning stove in Norfolk?

The total cost of a wood burning stove installation in Norfolk — including the stove, labour and any chimney lining — typically ranges from £1,500 to £3,500 depending on the stove you choose and the condition of your existing chimney. We provide free, detailed quotes so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need a HETAS registered installer in Norwich?

Yes — using a HETAS registered installer is strongly recommended and required under building regulations. HETAS installers can self-certify the installation and notify your local authority on your behalf, keeping you fully compliant with planning and insurance requirements.

Can I install a wood burning stove without an existing chimney?

Yes — it is possible to install a log burner without an existing chimney using a twin-wall flue system. Our team will assess your property during a free home survey and advise on the best solution for your home.

How often should I have my chimney swept in Norfolk?

We recommend having your chimney swept at least once a year for wood burning stoves, and twice a year if you use your stove heavily or burn coal. Regular chimney sweeping prevents the dangerous build-up of creosote and significantly reduces the risk of chimney fires.
