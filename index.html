Common questions

Woodburner Installation FAQs

Everything you need to know before booking your installation in Norwich or Norfolk.

How much does a wood burning stove installation cost in Norfolk?

A fully installed wood burning stove in Norfolk costs from £1,926 with Watmore's Woodburners — all inclusive. This covers the stove, flue liner, hearth preparation, HETAS certification and building regs notification. We provide a free detailed quote before any work begins.

Do you need planning permission for a log burner in Norfolk?

In most cases, no. Installing a wood burning stove falls under permitted development. However, if you live in a conservation area or listed building, additional checks apply. As HETAS registered installers we handle the building regulations notification on your behalf.

What areas of Norfolk do you cover?

We cover all of Norfolk — from Norwich and the Broads to the north Norfolk coast, the Brecks and west Norfolk. Towns we regularly serve include North Walsham, Fakenham, Cromer, Holt, Sheringham, Wymondham, Dereham, Great Yarmouth, King's Lynn, Wroxham, Aylsham and all surrounding villages.

How long does a woodburner installation take?

Most standard installations are completed in a single day. Jobs involving a twin-wall external flue may take two days. We'll always confirm the timescale with you upfront and leave your home completely clean and tidy.

Do you supply the stove as well as install it?

Yes — our packages are fully inclusive. We supply the stove, liner, fittings and all certification. You don't need to source anything separately. We'll advise on the best stove for your home and budget during your free home survey.

Are you HETAS registered?

Yes — Watmore's Woodburners are fully HETAS registered. This means all installations meet the highest UK safety standards, come with an official HETAS certificate, and satisfy the requirements of all major home insurers.
