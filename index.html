Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to install a wood burning stove in Norfolk?

The cost of a wood burning stove installation in Norfolk typically ranges from £1,500 to £3,500 depending on the stove chosen, the condition of your chimney, and any additional work required such as chimney lining. We provide free, detailed quotes so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need a HETAS registered installer?

Yes — using a HETAS registered installer is strongly recommended. HETAS installers can self-certify the installation, notifying your local council on your behalf. This ensures your installation is legal, safe and won't affect your home insurance.

Can I install a wood burning stove without a chimney?

Yes, it is possible using a twin-wall flue system. Our team can advise on the best solution for your property during a free home survey.

How often should I have my chimney swept?

It is recommended to have your chimney swept at least once a year if you burn wood, and twice a year if you burn coal or use your stove heavily. Regular sweeping prevents dangerous chimney fires.
