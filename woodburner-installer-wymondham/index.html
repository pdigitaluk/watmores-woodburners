Wood Burning Stoves in Wymondham

Wymondham is one of south Norfolk's most popular market towns, sitting just 9 miles south-west of Norwich. It's a town full of character — the historic Abbey, timber-framed buildings and broad mix of period and newer properties all make it a great area for woodburner installations. Many of the town's Victorian and Edwardian homes have original chimney flues that work perfectly with a modern stove and new liner.

We carry out woodburner installations throughout Wymondham and surrounding south Norfolk villages including Hethersett, Spooner Row, Wicklewood, Kimberley, Barnham Broom, Bawburgh and Long Stratton. Every installation is HETAS certified and building regulations compliant.
