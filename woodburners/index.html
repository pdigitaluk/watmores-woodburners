Common questions

Stove Installation FAQs

Everything you need to know before booking your installation in Norwich or Norfolk.

How much does a wood burning stove installation cost in Norfolk?

A fully installed wood burning stove in Norfolk typically costs between £1,500 and £3,500. This includes the stove, flue liner, hearth preparation, HETAS certification and building regs notification. We provide free, detailed quotes with no hidden charges.

How long does installation take?

Most standard installations are completed in a single day. More complex jobs — such as those requiring twin-wall flue systems — may take two days. We'll always give you a realistic timescale before we start.

Can I install a wood burner without a chimney?

Yes — we can install a twin-wall flue system through an external wall or through the roof. This is a clean, efficient solution that works well in modern properties across Norfolk. We'll assess your home during a free survey.

What size stove do I need?

As a rough guide, you need approximately 1kW of output per 14 cubic metres of room volume. However, insulation, ceiling height and heat loss all affect the calculation. We'll advise on the correct size during your free home survey.
