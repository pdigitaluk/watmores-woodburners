Chimney Sweeping in Cromer

We provide a professional, reliable chimney sweeping service throughout Cromer and the north Norfolk coast. Cromer's wealth of Victorian and Edwardian properties — with their period chimney stacks and original flues — makes it one of our busiest sweeping areas. Whether you have a newly installed stove with a flexible liner or an original open fireplace, we carry out a thorough clean and inspection and issue a sweep certificate on the day.

Annual sweeping is required by most UK home insurance policies for properties with solid fuel appliances. We also serve Sheringham, Holt, Overstrand, Northrepps, West Runton, East Runton and all surrounding north Norfolk coast villages.

Common questions

Cromer Chimney Sweep FAQs

How often should I have my chimney swept in Cromer?

At least once a year for wood burners and solid fuel appliances. Most UK home insurers require this as a policy condition. We issue a sweep certificate after every visit.

Do you sweep chimneys in Sheringham and Holt from Cromer?

Yes — Sheringham, Holt, Overstrand, West Runton, East Runton and all surrounding north Norfolk coast villages are within our regular service area.

Do I need a chimney sweep certificate in Cromer?

Most UK home insurers require evidence of annual sweeping. We issue an official sweep certificate on the day of every visit — valid for 12 months.

How long does a chimney sweep take in Cromer?

A standard chimney sweep takes around 45–60 minutes. We use dust sheets throughout and leave your home completely clean and tidy.

Can you sweep both stove flue liners and open fireplaces in Cromer?

Yes — we sweep all flue types including stainless steel flexible liners, clay flue pots and open brick chimneys for wood and solid fuel appliances.
